tunes🎧Christmas Update (dec 19)

In today’s update we have new website updates and improvements, and of course new commands for tunes (check out -help to try it yourself)

COMMANDS

-8ball (question) —> asks the 8ball a question
-allbots —> shows bots and their statuses -amazeme —> shows a random amazing picture lol
-anime (anime name) —> searches up anime by name -avatarcombine —> combines your avatar with another user
-badges —> shows all discord badges a user has -comment —> puts your text in a command
-dm (user) —> direct messages a user in the server with your message -editsnipe —> shows any message before it was edited
-facepalm —> gets a facepalm -fire —> sets your avatar on fire
-freeze —> freezes your avatar -invites —> shows how many invites you or an user has
-poll —> creates a poll in a channel -position —> shows the position of a member in a server
-respect —> pay respects to a person
-roast —> roast someone xx
-rps —> starts a game of Rock Paper Scissors
-ship —> ships users together
-shit —> covers you in shit
-suggest —> creates and sends your suggestion
-triggered —> triggered yourself or another user?
-urban —> search for something in the urban dictionary
-wikipedia —> search wiki for something WEBSITE
